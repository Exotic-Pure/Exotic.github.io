About Leadership

Exotic is built on experience and unfinished goals.

I previously led a Mythic progression guild and stepped away when my wife and I welcomed our baby. It was the right decision. Family comes first, but the drive to lead and push Cutting Edge never left.

Now I’m back with the time, focus, and stability to build something the right way.

The War Within was about rebuilding and laying a real foundation. Moving into Midnight, the objective is clear: earn our first Cutting Edge as a team and my first CE as a guild leader.

We’re not chasing hype. We’re building consistency. A stable roster. Clean comms. Real accountability. No drama. No ego. Just steady progression toward something that lasts.
